Welcome to Hangman (Console Version)!

In this Hangman, there are two modes playable

* Single Player * 
	In this mode, you will be given a random word to guess
	Tries: 9
	Hints: 3

* Two Players * 
	In this mode, one person will be designated to choose a word for the other person
	Make sure you dont look while they type it!!!
	
	Tries: Player 1 is able to choose how many tries Player 2 is able to get

	Hints: Player 1 is able to choose how many hints Player 2 is given

	The game continues as normal

* Exit * 
	Exit the game
